Why I made it?

This website mostly focuses on people starting to learn about fighter jets not falling down the pipeline of missinformation. So much information on the internet is full of purposeful misinformation and information that could give someone new a bias. I want to give new people information that should stop them from this, as well as giving them information on how to avoid that.
